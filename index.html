Our Story

Rooted in Tradition,
Crafted for You

A Sanctuary Born of Love

Serenity Ayurveda was founded in 2010 by Dr. Meera Nair, a third-generation Vaidya (Ayurvedic physician) who dreamed of bringing authentic, uncompromised Ayurvedic healing to the modern world.

What began as a small treatment room in Kerala has blossomed into a beloved wellness sanctuary — a place where ancient wisdom meets contemporary comfort, and where every guest is treated like family.

Today, we remain devoted to the same principles that guided our founders: purity of herbs, precision of technique, and the profound belief that true wellness begins within.
